Adventures and Destinations

TOKYO, JAPAN

Weather: Spring breeze, 73F

Tokyo is a city that never sits still, a place where the mornings brings peace, while nights burst alive with the neon lights and laughter.

Activites:

Visted Senso-ji Temple in Asakusa, the incense smoke brings good luck.

Ate a very good bowl of ramen in Shinjuku's Omoide Yokocho alley.

Shopped for anime collectibles in Akihabara, ready to put it along collection back at home.

One of the best cherry blossoms light up shows in Ueno Park.

Went on a car-ride through the streets in a skyline r34 GT-R.

PARIS, FRANCE

Weather: Sunny afternoon, 78F

Paris has a lot of history, walking along the cobblestone streets lined with cafes, museums, and boutiques. The air of fresh pastries has me wanting some.

Activites:

Visted the world famous art at the Louvre and Musee d'Orsay.

Enjoyed a nice baked corissants and coffee at a cafe located in Saint Germain.

I was also able to enjoy the Eiffel Tower sparkle at night, it is something i'll be looking forward to the next time I go again.

Being able to walk along the Champs Elysees for some window shopping was pretty cool, it reminded me of a couple of movie sense that were taken there.

At the end of the day, I enjoyed myself the first expensive wine bottle, with some cheese by the Seine River.

LONDON, ENLAND

Weather: Cloudy partly sunny, 60F

London where history and modernity, royal palaces, historic pubs, and red double decker buses.

Activites:

Witnessed the Changing of the Guard at Buckingham Palace.

Enjoyed a nice baked croissants and coffee at a cafe located in Saint Germain.

Rode the London Eye for Pernoramic City views.

I was able to enjoy traditional tea in a charming tea room.

I was able to ride on a red double decker bus through the city.

MEXICO CITY, MEXICO

Weather: Warm and sunny, 80F

Mexico City is somewhere where music can be heard everywhere, food venders in almost every block, candy is tasty, the city is full of surprises.

Activites:

I tried many did different tacos, all of them has a unique taste with some rice water with the food.

Enjoyed the ancient pyramids at Teotihuacan, it was a hot day, and heard stories about the pyramids.

Enjoyed live mariachi music, I was also able to learn how to dance it.

Being able to walk through the murals of Diego Rivera at Palacio de Bellas Artes.
